## Currencies and limits

## Supported fiat currencies

| Currency | Bank format | Minimum withdrawal | Processing time |
|---|---|---|---|
| GBP | Sort code + account number | $50 equivalent | 1–2 business days |
| USD | ABA routing + account number | $50 | 1–3 business days |
| EUR | IBAN + SWIFT/BIC | $50 equivalent | 2–3 business days |

All withdrawals are converted from USDC at the spot rate at the time Prudra's ops team processes the conversion. Exchange rate risk is carried by you between the time you request and the time conversion executes (typically within 4 hours).

## Minimum and maximum amounts

| Plan | Minimum | Maximum per withdrawal | Maximum per month |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hobby | $50 | $5,000 | $10,000 |
| Pro | $50 | $50,000 | $500,000 |
| Enterprise | $50 | Unlimited | Unlimited |

<Note>
  High-value withdrawals (>$50,000) may require additional KYC verification. Contact [support@prudra.com](mailto:support@prudra.com) to pre-approve large withdrawals.
</Note>

## Exchange rates

Prudra converts USDC to fiat at the mid-market rate at the time of conversion, minus a conversion fee:

| Plan | Conversion fee |
|---|---|
| Hobby | 1.0% |
| Pro | 0.5% |
| Enterprise | Negotiated |

The conversion fee is applied on top of any bank wire fees charged by the receiving bank.

## Bank wire fees

Most receiving banks charge a fee to receive international wires:

- UK banks: typically free for GBP wires
- US banks: typically $0–$15 per incoming wire
- EU banks: typically €0–€15 per SEPA wire

These fees are charged by your bank, not by Prudra.
